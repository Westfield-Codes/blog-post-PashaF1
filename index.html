Whether you're a drug kingpin or a weapons dealer, you want a fast car that keeps out the bullets and locks in the comfort. Of course, if you live in a place where hostilities are taking place, you will also need a bulletproof car.

But no one canceled the war and they drive with the fear that someone can shoot at the car with a gun, so a good option to protect yourself and your family would be this bulletproof car.

Look at the photo of this beautiful, stylish, super fast, and bulletproof car. Wait what!? Yes, you heard it right, the Audi rs7, apart from being so beautiful and fast, also has bulletproof.
